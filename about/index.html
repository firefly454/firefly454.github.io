About me....

I completed my doctoral dissertation on the biomechanics of hummingbird flight in December 2014 at the University of California, Berkeley, but am currently working on a career change into data science. I'm excited about data science because it fulfills my sense of curiosity - I enjoy playing with data and figuring out what it means! Prior to my graduate studies, I received a B.A. in Mathematics at New York University.
